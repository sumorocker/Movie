The Shinning, directed by Stanley Kubrick is a psychological thriller loosely based on a Steven King Novel. It takes place in Colorado at the Overlook hotel. Our protagonist Jack Torrance(Jack Nicholson) and his wife(Shelley Duvall) and son Danny(Danny Lloyd) take residence at the Overlook when Jack is accepted in the position of winter caretaker. A position he took in hopes that the isolation might break his writers block.

Jack's son Danny suffers from seizures that we learn are caused by a psychic sensitivity to people and the past, an ability that becomes known as Shinning. All the while Jack is frustratingly trying to overcome his writers block and cabin fever begins to set in causing him slowly to loose touch.

The Overlook itself is filled with a terrible history who's presence can still be felt. As Jack descends deeper into madness he encounters more and more of it until he himself becomes part of it. He sets out to murder his wife and child who must attempt to survive far from any help and cut off from any roads by the harsh winter weather.
